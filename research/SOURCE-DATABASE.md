## Concept Taxonomy

### Level 1: Strategic Themes

#### 1. Agentic AI
- **Definition:** Autonomous AI systems performing multi-step workflows independently
- **Key Characteristics:**
  - Execute complex tasks without human intervention
  - Make decisions within defined parameters
  - Coordinate with other agents and systems
  - Learn and adapt from outcomes
- **Sources:** 2025 CEO Study, Business Trends 2025, Scaling Gen AI
- **Statistics:** 61% active adoption, 46% scaling by end of 2025
- **Related Concepts:** Agent orchestration, autonomous decision-making, multi-agent systems

#### 2. Investment Acceleration
- **Definition:** Rapid increase in AI budgets and resource allocation
- **Key Metrics:**
  - AI investment growth >2x in next 2 years
  - Data strategy budget tripled (4% → 13%)
  - Self-funding models emerging
- **Sources:** 2025 CEO Study, 2025 CDO Study, Business Trends 2025
- **Drivers:** Investor pressure, competitive urgency, ROI expectations
- **Related Concepts:** Budget reallocation, self-funding AI, capital allocation

#### 3. ROI Expectations vs. Reality
- **Expectation:** 85% expect positive ROI by 2027
- **Reality:** Only 25% achieved expected ROI (past 3 years)
- **Gap Analysis:** 60-point expectation-execution gap
- **Sources:** 2025 CEO Study, Scaling Gen AI
- **Implications:** Need for realistic planning, risk management
- **Related Concepts:** Value realization, performance measurement, benefit tracking

#### 4. Readiness Gap
- **Definition:** Disconnect between AI ambition and organizational capability
- **Dimensions:**
  - Data readiness: 26% CDO confidence

  - Workforce: 31% require retraining
- **Sources:** All major studies
- **Root Causes:** Legacy systems, siloed operations, talent gaps
- **Related Concepts:** Organizational capability, maturity assessment, gap analysis
